Getting Started

What is Mobi-Cart?

The One-Stop Planner provides a variety of teaching resources through easy-to-follow, point-and-click menus. Teaching resources include:

  • Printable program resources
  • Printable lesson plans
  • Interactive Teacher's Edition, with links to program resources
  • ExamView® Test Generator, which allows you to create your own tests
  • Holt Calendar Planner, which allows you to schedule lesson plans and classroom activities for the year

Navigating through Mobi-Cart

Finding and Viewing a Resource

Resources are available through the One-Stop Planner menu system. To select and view a resource:

  1. From the One-Stop Planner home page, click any button.
    The menu for the selected category displays, with links to submenus or resources.
  2. Continue to click links, moving through submenus, until the link for the desired resource is found.
  3. Click the link representing the desired resource.
    The resource will open in a new window so you can view and/or print it.

Closing a Resource

Click the Close box in the upper right corner of the window containing the resource.
The resource window will close, returning you to the One-Stop Planner window.

Returning to the Table of Contents

From any submenu under the One-Stop Planner table of contents, click the Table of Contents link on the left side of the screen.
The table of contents will replace the contents of the active window.

Navigating an Alphabetical Menu

Some of the menus in the One-Stop Planner allow you to access information from a set of alphabetized entries. To do this:

  1. Underneath the menu header, click the letter that starts the name of the desired entry.
    A list of items that start with that letter will appear.
  2. Scroll through the list until the desired entry is found.
  3. Click the link representing the desired entry.
    A screen containing more information about the entry, including links to any associated resources, will appear.

One-Stop Planner Resources

Audio and Video Segments

Some program resources available through the One-Stop Planner Table of Contents may be audio or video segments. To learn how to access the audio and video segments, see Finding and Viewing a Resource.

To play the audio and video segments, you must have the correct version of the QuickTime™ player installed on your computer. To learn how to install the QuickTime™ player, see Installing Readers.

Installing Readers

Your One-Stop Planner includes all the readers and other plug-ins you need to view the resources that are included. To install a reader or other plug-in:

  1. Open the One-Stop Planner home page.
  2. Click to open the Viewers menu in the upper right corner of the screen.
  3. Click to select the desired reader from the menu.
    The installer for the desired reader will run.

Accessing Related Software

Exiting the One-Stop Planner

You may exit the One-Stop Planner at any time. To do this, click the Close box in the upper right corner of the window.
